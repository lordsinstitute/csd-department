How the AI
engine works

Three intelligent stages transform your raw skill ratings into ranked career matches with personalized guidance. No guesswork — pure mathematics.

1

Skill Vectorization

Your ratings across 120+ skills are normalized to a 0–1 scale and arranged into a precise mathematical vector — ready for AI comparison.

2

Cosine Similarity Matching

Your vector is compared against 50+ career profiles using cosine similarity — the same method used in modern NLP and recommendation systems.

3

Gap Analysis + Roadmap

Core and supporting skills are scored separately. Gaps are identified and a career-specific 3 and 6 month learning roadmap is generated for you.
